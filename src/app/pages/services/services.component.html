Tools for Market Ops and Delivery

To streamline field operations and empower your teams, the system offers dedicated mobile apps for sales agents and delivery personnel.

Customer-Facing Tools

A retailers’ mobile app keeps your customers connected with your Qwipo distribution system, empowering them to place and track their orders, manage payments and returns, and more.

The Vendors Mobile App

As an SME or a microbusiness, you can manage distribution operations on the go with Qwipo’s Vendor Mobile App. Designed to let you manage operations from anywhere, anytime, the native Android and iOS app allows you to keep tabs on customer payments, sales, inventory, and product returns. Harness upselling and cross-selling opportunities, implement main and secondary pricing strategies and gain vital market intelligence for optimal pricing tactics. Finance your customers, schedule delivery vans, and maintain 24/7 customer engagement.

The Business Retailers Mobile App

Connect seamlessly to QWIPO’s extensive marketplace of vendors and sellers via this native mobile app. Retailers can explore a vast array of products, benefit from AI-driven recommendations, execute digital payments, leverage diverse credit options, forecast demand, and operate their businesses more effectively and efficiently.

The Sales Agents App

Enable your sales team with the Field Agents Mobile App, allowing them to place orders, recommend products, and manage schemes on the move. Track their market visits, monitor time spent with each retailer, and boost overall business efficiency.

The Smart Delivery App

Optimize your delivery operations with real-time load calculations and routing adjustments based on traffic conditions. Our cutting-edge technology helps you save time, reduce fuel consumption, and minimize your carbon footprint. Delivery agents can also track payment settlements and returns, all within the same app.
